About Me

Grayson Wasson is a Games + Simulations major mainly delving into the art track. They are also learning how to code websites like this in day to day life. Grayson has been doing digital art of 7 years and has learned how to do many different styles. They have had a GPA average of 4.0 since freshman year of high school. They went to DRL Butler Tech campus ever since their sophomore year of high school. They've earned things like the READY award chosen by teachers twice during high school. They were the resin printer owner for one year at their vocational school. Grayson has even earned an associate's in electromechanical engineering and technologies through Sinclair Community College.
